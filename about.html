Introduction to Forebear Find

About Forebear Find - Expert Italian & European Genealogy Services

Welcome to Forebear Find, where your family's past comes alive through meticulous research and compelling storytelling. We specialize in Italian ancestry research, European genealogy, African American family history, and custom family narratives that preserve your heritage for generations to come.

Our Specialized Genealogy Services

  • Italian dual citizenship research and vital records retrieval from all 20 Italian regions
  • European ancestry research including Italian, Scottish, Irish, and Eastern European lineages
  • African American genealogy using specialized research techniques and historical records
  • Immigration and naturalization records research (Ellis Island, ship manifests, citizenship papers)
  • Custom family history books and beautifully written ancestral narratives
  • Vital records procurement with proper certifications and apostilles

Meet Rocco DeLuca - Lead Genealogist & Founder

Rocco DeLuca is a professional genealogist based in Vernal, Utah, specializing in Italian and European ancestry research. With decades of experience navigating Italian archives, church records, and government offices, Rocco has helped countless families discover their roots and obtain the documentation needed for Italian dual citizenship applications.

Rocco's expertise extends beyond simple record retrieval. He combines traditional archival research with innovative problem-solving techniques to break through genealogical brick walls and uncover hidden family connections. His passion for storytelling transforms raw genealogical data into engaging narratives that capture the essence of your ancestors' lives.

From medieval Italian villages to American immigration records, Rocco's research has traced lineages across continents and centuries, reuniting families with their heritage and helping preserve these precious connections for future generations.

Our Research Approach

At Forebear Find, we combine traditional genealogical research methods with innovative problem-solving. We access historical records from multiple sources including:

  • Italian state archives (Archivi di Stato)
  • Municipal civil records offices (Uffici di Stato Civile)
  • Parish church registers (Registri Parrocchiali)
  • Ellis Island immigration databases
  • U.S. Census records and vital statistics
  • Military service records
  • Naturalization and citizenship documents
  • Freedmen's Bureau records for African American research
