Our Genealogy Services

At ForebearFind, we offer specialized genealogy services that help you uncover your family's legacy, no matter how complex your heritage may be.

African American Heritage

Reconstructing family lines post-Emancipation using Freedmen’s Bureau records, census data, land deeds, and oral histories.

Italian Dual Citizenship

Document recovery, translation, and legal support for clients pursuing jure sanguinis recognition through ancestral lines.

Noble Lineages & Land Records

Investigating aristocratic titles, medieval land holdings, and feudal inheritance across Europe and Spanish America.

Custom Research Projects

Tailored solutions for client-specific needs, from archival deep dives to genetic genealogy reports.
