Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Italian genealogy research take?

Timelines vary depending on complexity and record availability. Simple family verifications may take 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive dual citizenship packages typically require 6-8 weeks.

Can you help me with Italian dual citizenship?

Yes! We specialize in gathering genealogical documentation for Italian citizenship applications. We verify unbroken lineage and compile all required civil records.

What if my family records were lost or destroyed?

We use alternative research methods including immigration records, census data, naturalization papers, and church documents to reconstruct your family line.

Do you search records for ancestors from all Italian regions?

Yes, we research families from throughout Italy. We have connections in all regions and can access regional archives.
