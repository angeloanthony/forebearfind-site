About Rocco

Hello! I’m Rocco DeLuca, the founder of Forebear Find. I specialize in uncovering family stories hidden in old documents — especially those that have been overlooked, misfiled, or forgotten.

My background blends historical research, technology, and storytelling. I’ve worked extensively on:

  • 📜 African American genealogy during Reconstruction
  • 🇮🇹 Italian and European nobility research
  • ⚖️ Legal and forensic cases involving land and probate records
  • 🌍 Dual citizenship documentation and immigration pathways

Whether I’m digging through parish archives in Calabria or tracing freedmen communities in Texas, I treat every family’s history with care, dignity, and precision.

At Forebear Find, we’re more than just genealogy researchers — we’re story-seekers, problem-solvers, and memory restorers. As a boutique and deeply personal research service, we specialize in uncovering family stories through meticulous document analysis, historical context, and compassion.

We Specialize In:

  • African American Genealogy – tracing ancestry through slavery, Reconstruction, and beyond
  • Italian & European Nobility – uncovering noble lineage and verifying dual citizenship
  • Forensic Lineage Proof – supporting court cases, probate claims, and land disputes
  • Immigration Research – documenting ancestors’ journeys and naturalization paths
  • Life Story Books – producing family tree histories in modular PDF or print-ready form

How We Find Your Ancestors

  • Step 1: We gather clues from your family information or oral history.
  • Step 2: We search national archives, census records, immigration files, and church documents.
  • Step 3: We analyze DNA results (if available) and match them to historical patterns.
  • Step 4: We build you a narrative report, family tree, and source citations.
